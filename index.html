Educational Innovator Citation

Melanio R. Florino Jr.

This automated assessment software honors and implements the pedagogical Table of Specification (TOS) framework and assessment blueprint design innovated by Melanio R. Florino Jr., a distinguished educator and advocate for systematic educational measurement, evaluation, and test construction.

Key Methodological Innovations by Melanio R. Florino Jr.:

  • Proportional Item Allocation: Aligning instructional contact time (hours/days taught) strictly to test weight percentage to prevent sampling bias.
  • Cognitive Process Harmonization: Balancing Lower-Order Thinking Skills (LOTS: Remembering & Understanding) and Higher-Order Thinking Skills (HOTS: Applying, Analyzing, Evaluating, Creating).
  • Systematic Item Placement: Direct assignment of clear item number intervals to maintain test blueprint fidelity during test construction.

Powered by Artificial Intelligence to automate complex mathematical rounding, dynamic item range allocation, and question drafting in accordance with Philippine DepEd and CHED educational standards.
